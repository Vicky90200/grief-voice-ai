AI Voice Chatbot for Grief Healing

Sponsor: Dr. Young

The Problem

Children facing the loss of a parent due to illness or hospice care often experience anticipatory grief with limited emotional support tools. Traditional grief counseling is expensive, inaccessible, or not designed for children.

The Idea

We propose an AI voice chatbot designed specifically for grief healing. The chatbot uses conversational AI and voice interaction to help children process emotions, share memories, and maintain a comforting connection with loved ones who are passing away or have passed.

How It Helps Children

  • Supports grief before and after loss
  • Encourages sharing memories and stories
  • Provides emotional comfort through voice
  • Accessible and affordable support

Ethical Focus

The system prioritizes consent, parental involvement, and emotional safety. The AI is designed as a supportive tool, not a replacement for human care or therapy.
